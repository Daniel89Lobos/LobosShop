Store Terms

These store terms describe the basic conditions for ordering from Lobos Shop. They are written to be clear and practical for customers.

Products and pricing

  • Product listings, availability, and pricing can change over time.
  • Prices are shown in SEK unless another currency is clearly stated.
  • We do our best to keep product descriptions and stock information accurate, but obvious errors may be corrected.

Orders and payment

  • An order is submitted when checkout is completed successfully through Stripe.
  • If a product is unavailable, incorrectly priced, or cannot be fulfilled, we may cancel the order and issue a refund.
  • Available payment options may include card and other methods offered through Stripe Checkout for your region and order.

Shipping and delivery

  • We currently ship within Sweden only during the first launch phase.
  • A flat shipping cost is added during checkout.
  • Delivery estimates are not guarantees and may vary based on carrier performance and destination.

Returns and customer accounts

  • Returns are handled according to the process described on the returns page.
  • If you create an account, you are responsible for keeping your login details secure.
  • Orders placed while signed in may appear in your account history for easier tracking.

Store content and contact

  • Store content, branding, product text, and images remain the property of Lobos Shop unless stated otherwise.
  • Questions about an order, policy, or support issue can be sent through the contact page.
  • These terms are intended to complement, not reduce, rights granted by applicable consumer law.
